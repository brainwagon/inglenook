How to Play

Inglenook Sidings is a classic railway shunting puzzle. Your goal is to assemble a specific train by moving cars between sidings.

  • Move to: Use the A/B/C/D buttons to drive the locomotive (and any coupled cars) to a siding.
  • Couple: Pick up the nearest car on the current siding.
  • Decouple: Drop all coupled cars onto the current siding.
  • The locomotive can only travel between siding A and the other sidings (B, C, D) — not directly between B, C, and D.
  • Each siding has limited capacity. You cannot move if there isn’t enough room.

Assemble the target cars (shown at the top-left) on siding A in the correct order to win. Try to do it in as few moves as possible!
